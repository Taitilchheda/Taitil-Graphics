# Product Design Document (PDD)
## Taitil Graphics – Creative Studio Portfolio Platform
TaitilGraphics.com showcases a modern graphic design portfolio site focused on freelance services like branding, web design, and illustration. The platform prioritizes visual storytelling, portfolio discovery, service clarity, and lead generation.
CORE CONCEPT & GOALS
The site serves as a creative showcase for freelancers targeting SMEs and premium clients. Key goals include high engagement via visuals, seamless navigation, and strong CTAs for consultations.
COLOR PALETTE & TYPOGRAPHY
Primary palette uses neutral tones (grays, whites) with subtle brand accents (greens/blues).
Typography is limited to two fonts:
• Sans-serif for body (e.g., Outfit)
• Serif for headings (e.g., Cormorant Garamond)
VISUAL ELEMENTS & IMAGERY
• Full-width hero galleries
• Hover colorization effects
• High-resolution portfolio visuals
• Minimal line art and custom icons
PAGE LAYOUT & NAVIGATION
Sticky navigation with Home, Services, Portfolio, About, Contact.
Grid-based layouts with ample whitespace.
Mobile-first responsive design.
KEY SECTIONS
Hero – Immersive gallery with CTA
Services – Card-based service blocks
Portfolio – Gallery-first case studies
Process – Visual timeline
Contact – Conversion-optimized form
RESPONSIVENESS & UX
Optimized for mobile, tablet, desktop.
Fast load times via optimized images.
Accessible navigation and SEO-friendly structure.
TECHNICAL NOTES
Likely CMS: WordPress, Webflow, or Squarespace
Optimized assets, SEO metadata, and analytics integration.
