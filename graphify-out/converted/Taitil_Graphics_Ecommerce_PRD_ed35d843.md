# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Taitil Graphics – E‑Commerce Marketplace Platform
### 1. Product Overview
Taitil Graphics aims to build a scalable multi‑vendor e‑commerce marketplace similar to Amazon and Meesho, enabling sellers to list products and customers to discover and purchase them seamlessly.
### 2. Vision & Goals
Vision: Empower sellers with a simple yet powerful marketplace.
Goals:
• Easy seller onboarding
• Smooth customer checkout
• Secure payments
• Scalable architecture
### 3. Target Users
• Customers: Mobile‑first online shoppers
• Sellers: Small businesses, D2C brands, creators
• Admin: Platform operators
### 4. Core Features
• User authentication (Customer, Seller, Admin)
• Product catalog & search
• Cart & checkout
• Payments & refunds
• Order & inventory management
• Reviews & ratings
• Admin dashboard
### 5. Non‑Functional Requirements
• Performance: <2s load time
• Security: HTTPS, encrypted data
• Scalability: Cloud‑native
• Reliability: 99.9% uptime
### 6. Technology Stack
Frontend: React / Next.js
Backend: Node.js / FastAPI
Database: PostgreSQL
Payments: Razorpay / Stripe
Hosting: AWS / GCP
### 7. Monetization
• Commission per sale
• Seller subscription plans
• Featured listings
### 8. MVP Scope
Included: Login, product listing, checkout, payments, admin panel
Excluded: AI recommendations, live chat (future)
### 9. Risks & Mitigation
• Payment failures – Multiple gateways
• Seller fraud – KYC verification
• Scaling issues – Auto‑scaling infrastructure
### 10. Future Roadmap
• AI product recommendations
• Influencer storefronts
• Regional language support
